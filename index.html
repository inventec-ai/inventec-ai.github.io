Hiring

Hiring - AI Engineer

Job description

An AI Engineer is responsible for developing AI prototypes for the future and bringing innovative AI solutions to manufacturing. To be successful in this role, one important requirement is the innate curiosity for what’s new, and a strong ability to learn new technologies quickly. The individual must possess deep, hand-on technical skills and be familiar with latest AI development tools and environments. He or she will involve in all stages of R&D: defining problems, designing and training AI/ML systems, and testing and analyzing solutions. Last but not least, he or she will work across multiple groups in the company and potentially with outside partners. Hence, good communication skill and teamwork is critical for this role.

Required qualifications

  • MS/PhD in Electrical Engineering/Computer Science/Machine learning/Statistics/Math/Physics/Neuroscience or equivalent practical experience
  • 2+ years hands-on software or Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning/Computer Vision/Nature Language Processing (AI/ML/CV/NLP) development experience
  • Self motivated, communicative, team oriented

Preferred experience

  • Proven experience with deep learning frameworks such as PyTouch, TensorFlow, Caffe, Torch
  • Fluency in Python and/or C/C++
  • Experience within IoT, Big Data and/or Industry 4.0
